Interesting Topics

Wenjun Zhang
Dec 28, 2019 1 min read

This post contains a list of topics that I am interested in or that I find important, though some of them could be at introductory level. When having enough time, I intend to make literature survey about them and write reviews or pedagogical introductions for freshman in AMO research, as well as for myself to go over through them.

Statistical mechanics / Quantum information

  • Integrability of quantum systems
    • (Algebraic) Bethe ansantz
    • Yang–Baxter equation
    • Reading list:
      • Algebraic Aspects of Bethe-Ansatz (arXiv:hep-th/9404013)
      • A pedagogical introduction to quantum integrability … (arXiv:1501.06805)
      • On the Integrability of the Rabi Model (arXiv:1103.2461)
      • Integrable time-dependent quantum Hamiltonians (arXiv:1711.09945)
      • Introduction to the Bethe ansatz (I, II and III) (arXiv:cond-mat/9809162)
  • Unitary group
    • Haar measure
    • Circular unitary ensemble (CUE)
    • Quantum $t$-design
    • Reading list:
      • Quantum signatures in of chaos
      • Exact and approximate unitary 2-designs and their application to fidelity estimation (doi:10.1103/PhysRevA.80.012304)

Atomic physics

  • Spin-orbit coupling
  • Exchange/Super-exchange interaction

Miscellaneous

  • Fluctuation dissipation theory.
